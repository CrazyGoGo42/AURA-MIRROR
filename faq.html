Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Aura Mirror products and services.

What makes Aura Mirror different from regular mirrors?

expand_more

Aura Mirror combines advanced AI technology with a high-quality mirror to provide personalized beauty analysis and recommendations. Unlike conventional mirrors, Aura Mirror can analyze your skin condition, recommend products, provide AR makeup previews, and adjust lighting based on different environmentsâ€”all through a sleek, integrated display.

How do I choose the right Aura Mirror model for me?

expand_more

We offer three models to suit different needs and budgets:

  • Aura Essential: Perfect for beginners with core skin analysis and product recommendation features.
  • Aura Premium: Includes all Essential features plus AR makeup preview and advanced lighting scenarios.
  • Aura Pro: Our most advanced model with all features plus voice control, enhanced AI, and premium design elements.

Visit our Mirrors page to compare all models in detail.

Do I need to download an app to use Aura Mirror?

expand_more

No, Aura Mirror works as a standalone device without requiring a separate app. All features are accessible through the mirror's interface. However, we do offer a companion app that allows you to access your skin analysis history, saved makeup looks, and product recommendations on your mobile device.

How accurate is the skin analysis technology?

expand_more

Our AI skin analysis technology has been developed with dermatologists and beauty experts and has a 95% accuracy rate when identifying common skin concerns such as dryness, redness, blemishes, and fine lines. The technology improves over time as it learns your skin's patterns through regular use.

Does Aura Mirror need an internet connection?

expand_more

Aura Mirror requires Wi-Fi connection for full functionality, including product recommendations, software updates, and cloud storage of your beauty profile. Basic mirror functionality and some analysis features will work offline, but for the complete experience, a stable internet connection is recommended.

How does Aura Mirror protect my privacy?

expand_more

Privacy is a top priority at Aura Mirror. Your facial and skin data is processed securely and is never shared with third parties without your explicit consent. All image processing is done locally on the device when possible, and any data sent to our secure cloud servers is encrypted. You can review and delete your data at any time through your account settings.

What is your return policy?

expand_more

We offer a 30-day satisfaction guarantee. If you're not completely satisfied with your Aura Mirror, you can return it within 30 days of delivery for a full refund. The product must be in its original packaging and in new condition. Return shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer unless the return is due to a defect or error on our part.

Do you ship internationally?

expand_more

Yes, we ship to most countries worldwide. International customers may be responsible for customs duties, taxes, and fees imposed by their country's customs agency. These charges are not included in our shipping costs and will be collected upon delivery. Please check our Shipping Information page for country-specific details.

How do I track my order?

expand_more

Once your order ships, you'll receive a confirmation email with a tracking number and a link to monitor your delivery. You can also track your order by logging into your account on our website and navigating to the Order History section.

Can I change or cancel my order?

expand_more

Orders can be modified or canceled within 2 hours of placement. After this window, we begin processing orders for shipment and cannot guarantee changes. Please contact our customer service team as soon as possible if you need to make changes to your order.

Do you offer warranty on Aura Mirrors?

expand_more

All Aura Mirror products come with a standard 1-year limited warranty that covers manufacturing defects. The Aura Premium and Pro models include an extended 2-year warranty. We also offer an optional protection plan that covers accidental damage and extends coverage up to 3 years.
