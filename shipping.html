Everything you need to know about our shipping policies, delivery options, and returns.

Processing Time

Orders are typically processed within 1-2 business days. During peak seasons or promotional periods, processing may take up to 3 business days. Once your order has shipped, you will receive a confirmation email with tracking information.

Mirror Installation

Our Aura Mirrors come with comprehensive installation instructions. For Premium and Pro models, we offer White Glove Delivery service, which includes professional installation and setup of your mirror. This service is complimentary with your mirror purchase.

Customs, Duties, and Taxes

International orders may be subject to import duties, taxes, and customs processing fees. These charges are the responsibility of the recipient and are not included in our shipping costs. Please check with your local customs office for more information before placing your order.

Returns & Exchanges

Return Policy

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If for any reason you are not, we offer a 30-day return policy for most products. Aura Mirror devices come with a 30-day satisfaction guarantee, allowing you to return the product for a full refund if you're not completely satisfied.

Return Eligibility

  • Items must be in their original, unopened packaging
  • Products must be in new, unused condition
  • Original receipt or proof of purchase is required
  • Return shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer unless the return is due to our error
  • Personalized or custom products cannot be returned unless defective

Exchange Process

If you'd like to exchange an item for a different size, color, or model, please initiate a return and place a new order for the desired item. This ensures the fastest processing time.

How to Initiate a Return

  1. Log into your account and navigate to Order History
  2. Select the order containing the item(s) you wish to return
  3. Click "Return Items" and follow the prompts
  4. Print the return shipping label (if applicable)
  5. Package the item(s) securely in their original packaging
  6. Attach the return shipping label and drop off at the designated carrier

Alternatively, you can contact our customer service team at support@auramirror.com for assistance with your return.

Refund Processing

Once we receive and inspect your return, we'll process your refund within 5-7 business days. Refunds will be issued to the original payment method used for the purchase. Please note that it may take an additional 2-5 business days for the refund to appear in your account, depending on your financial institution.

Order Tracking

How to Track Your Order

  1. Logging into your account on our website
  2. Navigating to the "Order History" section
  3. Selecting the order you wish to track
  4. Clicking on the "Track Shipment" button
