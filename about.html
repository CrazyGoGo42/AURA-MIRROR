Our Story

The vision and people behind Aura Mirror

Redefining Beauty Technology

At Aura Mirror, we believe that beauty and technology should work in harmony to enhance your natural features and simplify your daily routine. Founded in 2020 by three visionary entrepreneurs, our team came together with a shared passion for creating smart mirrors that understand and respond to individual beauty needs.

What began as an ambitious project between three friends has now grown into an innovative company at the intersection of AI, beauty, and wellness. Our revolutionary smart mirrors have transformed how people approach skincare and makeup, providing personalized insights and recommendations that were previously only available through professional consultations.

Our Mission

To empower individuals with personalized beauty intelligence that enhances their natural beauty and simplifies their daily routines through cutting-edge AI technology.

Our Journey

2020

The Beginning

Joline, Duy, and Daniel met during a tech innovation summit and discovered their shared vision to revolutionize beauty technology. Aura Mirror was born from their combined expertise.

2021

First Prototype

After a year of intensive development, our team created the first working prototype and secured seed funding from leading venture capital firms.

2022

Market Launch

We launched our first product, the Aura Essential Mirror, to critical acclaim and overwhelming customer response, establishing ourselves as leaders in beauty tech.

2023

Expansion & Growth

We expanded our product line with the Premium and Pro models, and established partnerships with leading beauty brands while growing our team to 25+ employees.

Today

Innovation Continues

We continue to push the boundaries of what's possible, developing new features and improving our AI to better serve our growing global community of users.
