Our Story

The vision and people behind Aura Mirror

Redefining Beauty Technology

At Aura Mirror, we believe that beauty and technology should work in harmony to enhance your natural features and simplify your daily routine. Founded in 2020, our team of engineers, beauty experts, and designers came together with a shared vision: to create smart mirrors that understand and respond to individual beauty needs.

What began as a startup in a small San Francisco studio has now grown into an innovative company at the intersection of AI, beauty, and wellness. Our revolutionary smart mirrors have transformed how people approach skincare and makeup, providing personalized insights and recommendations that were previously only available through professional consultations.

Our Mission

To empower individuals with personalized beauty intelligence that enhances their natural beauty and simplifies their daily routines.

Sophia Chen, Founder & CEO
